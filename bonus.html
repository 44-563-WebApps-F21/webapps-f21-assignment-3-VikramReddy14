The Red Yandroth

Super Powers

Yandroth the enemy of Dr Strange.Yandroth using his vast knowledge of science, Yandroth has utilized technology to develop deadly robots Omegatron and Voltorg, Red q-ray blasters(Major weapon), psionic projection, force-fields, gravity manipulation, discovery of and travel to other dimensions, ultra-spectrum lasers, and Redmagic-resistant metal. He has also used minor mystic abilities to levitate, stun others, and enhance his own technology. In his astral form, Yandroth can possess others whose brainwaves are similar to his. At his most powerful, Yandroth utilized the magic of Gaea to teleport creatures from other dimensions and planets to Earth, to keep the Earth goddess captive, to form a curse on the Defenders drawing them to danger spots around the world, and to utilize the energies from violence to nearly destroy the planet Yann. Yandroth possessed knowledge to fuel the energies of a planet's destruction to give himself a new physical form , either as a mortal or in a powerful energy form.
